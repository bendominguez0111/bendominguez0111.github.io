Now

I lead engineering at Gambly, a sports-betting copilot — a ten-engineer team across product, AI, and infrastructure. I built the AI platform behind it: the image-to-betslip system that turns a screenshot of any bet into a one-tap betslip, and the multi-agent chat system that powers GamblyBot across web, iMessage, Discord, and Telegram. Day to day that means agent orchestration, evals, durable execution, and the unglamorous data plumbing that makes all of it trustworthy.

I'm also the founder of Huddlevision, where we build computer vision for football — field registration, player detection and tracking, and the speed metrics behind Tracking Football's recruiting data.
